Welcome to Hot or Cold!

Directions:

  1. Every time, you start with the number 50.
  2. You have 5 chances to guess the number.
  3. Unless you manage to get your number in the very hot range. In this case, no guesses are subtracted while your number is "very hot".
  4. On each turn, you will choose to add or subtract a certain amount from the number.
  5. You cannot go above 100 or below 1. If you try, you will get an error message and no guesses will be subtracted, nor your guess modified.
  6. After you modify your guess, you will be told how hot or cold your guess is. This indicates how close your guess is to the answer.
  7. If you guess the number within 5 guesses, it will tell you that you won!
  8. If you want to be told the answer, you can press the "commit" button.
  9. If you want to play again, you can press the "reset" button.
  10. Click "go back" when you are ready to start.
  11. If you want to see these instructions again, you can press "show instructions".

Have fun!
